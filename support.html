Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my subscription?

To manage your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select Castellers
  5. Make changes or cancel as needed

How do I restore my purchases?

Your purchases are automatically restored when you sign in with the same Apple ID. If you're having trouble, try:

  1. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the purchase
  2. Restart the app
  3. If the issue persists, contact us using the information below

I'm having technical issues with the app

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Make sure you're using the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection
  • If the problem continues, please contact us
