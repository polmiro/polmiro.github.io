Privacy Policy for Castellers

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Introduction

Castellers ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, we collect and process transaction data through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. This includes:

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Transaction identifiers
  • Product identifiers

This information is used solely to provide and manage your subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and manage your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase data in accordance with their privacy policy. For more information, visit RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy practices are governed by their privacy policy, available at Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your purchase and subscription data is stored securely by RevenueCat and Apple. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Manage your subscriptions through the App Store

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
